The project includes a ready-to-use annotation package with 240 public items: 160 edit-explanation faithfulness items and 80 counterfactual edit-simulatability items. The guidelines cover edit correspondence, target consistency, operation consistency, error-type consistency, rule correctness, evidence correctness, specificity, behavioral predictiveness, overall faithfulness, and helpfulness. However, no real annotator labels have been collected in this environment. We therefore report no human correlation, no top-1 human preference, and no agreement statistic. Double annotation is required before making claims about human-perceived faithfulness or learner helpfulness.
